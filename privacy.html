      • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics, including interactions with features, session durations, and app performance.

      • Log Data: Our servers automatically collect error logs and technical reports in case of system failures.
    3. Third-Party Data Collection
      • We integrate third-party APIs, including OpenAI, Snapi LLC, and Finazon, to fetch stock-related data, news, and market sentiment.
      • These providers may collect and process data according to their privacy policies.
      • Apple’s in-app subscription system handles payments, and we do not store or process payment details.

  3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
    • Service Providers: Cloud storage providers (e.g., Firebase), payment processors (Apple, RevenueCat), and analytics tools.

    • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
