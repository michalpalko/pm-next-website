Privacy Policy Statement

Effective Date: October 1, 2025

PM Next s.r.o. (“Company”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use Finnext: AI Stock Forecasts (the “App”). By using the App, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

  1. Information We Collect
    1. Information You Provide
      • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and password.
      • Subscription Information: If you purchase a subscription, we collect data regarding your subscription plan, billing details, and renewal status, managed through Apple’s in-app subscription system and RevenueCat SDK.
      • User Inputs for Forecasts: When generating forecasts or reports, you may provide stock ticker symbols and optional details.
      • Day Trading Ideas: These are prepared and selected daily by our AI systems and delivered equally to all users. They are not based on user inputs.
      • Finnext Academy: When using Academy features (such as lessons, tutorials, quizzes, or progress tracking), we may collect your activity data (e.g., completed modules, scores, or progress) to enable personalized access to educational materials.
    2. Automatically Collected Information
      • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics such as feature interactions, session durations, andperformance data.
      • Device Information: Device model, OS version, unique identifiers.
      • Log Data: Crash reports and technical error logs.
    3. Third-Party Data Collection

      We integrate third-party APIs and SDKs to deliver forecasts, Day Trading Ideas, Finnext Academy, subscriptions, and analytics. These providers may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

      • AI Providers: We use OpenAI and xAI's Grok API to process anonymized inputs and generate AI-powered forecasts, Day Trading Ideas, and selected Academy materials.
      • Market Data Providers: Snapi LLC and Finazonsupply stock market data, sentiment, and news.
      • Payments & Subscriptions: Apple's and Google's in-app purchase systems and RevenueCat manage billing; we do not store payment card details.
      • Analytics & Storage: Firebase and similar services are used for secure storage, analytics, and fraud prevention.
    4. Device Identifiers and Usage Data
      • Our application collects and uses certain device identifiers, such as your device’s Android ID, Advertising ID (if available), device model, operating system, and IP address. These identifiers are collected automatically and are used for:
        • App functionality, including authentication, cloud data storage, and configuration delivery.
        • Account management to identify your device when you sign in, restore purchases, or access your account across devices.
        • Analytics and performance monitoring to understand app usage and improve stability.
        • Fraud prevention, security, and compliance to detect and prevent fraudulent activity.
      • We share these identifiers with trusted third-party service providers that power our app, including:
        • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore Database, Remote Config, Storage) for authentication, data storage, configuration, analytics, and crash diagnostics.
        • Superwall for paywall display, subscription management, and analytics.
        • RevenueCat for in-app purchase management and subscription verification.
      • These service providers may store the data in accordance with their privacy policies:
        • Firebase Privacy Policy
        • Superwall Privacy Policy
        • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
      • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use collected data to:

    • Authenticate users and manage accounts.
    • Generate and deliver forecasts, Day Trading Ideas, and educational content from Finnext Academy.
    • Track user progress within Finnext Academy to provide continuity of learning.
    • Provide customer support.
    • Monitor usage, performance, and prevent fraud or misuse.
    • Ensure compliance with app store policies and applicable laws.
  3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share data with:

    • Service Providers: Cloud storage, analytics, AI service providers (OpenAI, Grok), and payment processors.
    • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
    • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  4. Data Retention
    • Account Data: Retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of deletion.
    • Subscription Data: Retained as legally required (e.g., for tax compliance).
    • Generated AI Content: Forecasts, Day Trading Ideas, and Academy progress data are securely stored in Firebase and accessible only by you. They may be anonymized for analytics and improvements.
  5. Security Measures

    We implement industry-standard safeguards:

    • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
    • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel access sensitive data.
    • Monitoring: Fraud detection and misuse prevention measures are in place.
  6. User Rights

    Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to:

    • Access & Portability: Request a copy of your stored data.
    • Correction: Request updates or corrections to your data.
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.
    • Objection & Restriction: Limit or object to processing in certain circumstances.

    To exercise your rights, contact support@pm-next.com.

  7. International Data Transfers

    We may transfer data across jurisdictions in compliance with GDPR and other applicable privacy laws, using safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

  8. AI Processing and Limitations
    • Day Trading Ideas are generated internally by AI tools and distributed equally to all users, not based on individual inputs.
    • Forecasts and Academy materials may incorporate outputs from third-party AI models (OpenAI, Grok).
    • AI outputs may be inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated.
    • AI processing does not create a financial, investment, or advisory relationship between you and the Company.
  9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance. When legally required, we will notify you of changes.

  10. Contact Information
    • If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@pm-next.com
